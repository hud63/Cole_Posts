# LinkedIn Hashtag Research: Retirement Abroad & International Retirement
**Research Date:** October 26, 2025
**Focus:** REAL hashtags used on LinkedIn for retirement abroad and expat content
**Data Source:** 100 actual LinkedIn posts scraped from LinkedIn + Instagram hashtag statistics

---

## Executive Summary

This research analyzed 100 REAL LinkedIn posts about retirement, expat life, financial freedom, and international living to identify which hashtags are ACTUALLY being used on LinkedIn (not just popular on Instagram).

**Data Collected:**
- 50 posts from #retirement, #retireabroad, #expatlife, #retirementplanning, #internationalliving searches
- 50 posts from #financialfreedom, #financialplanning, #investing, #FIRE, #digitalnomad searches
- Additionally scraped Instagram hashtag statistics for 22 retirement/expat hashtags

**Key Findings:**
1. **LinkedIn hashtag usage is DIFFERENT from Instagram** - LinkedIn uses more professional/industry tags
2. **#retireabroad is RARELY used on LinkedIn** - only appeared in 5 posts out of 100
3. **Financial/professional hashtags dominate** - #financialplanning, #retirement, #wealth more common than location/lifestyle tags
4. **Many posts use NO hashtags** or very few (2-3 max) on LinkedIn
5. **Generic tags win** - #retirement, #expatlife, #financialfreedom used most frequently
