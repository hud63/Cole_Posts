## üìà KEY INSIGHTS FROM VERIFIED DATA

### What We Learned:

1. **Specificity Beats Breadth**
   - Tax professionals use SPECIFIC hashtags (#ExpatTax) over generic ones (#Tax)
   - Country-specific tags perform well (#CanadianTax, #AustralianTax)
   - Service-specific tags signal expertise (#AccountingForExpats)

2. **Professional Credibility Matters**
   - #CPA appears frequently for credibility
   - #TaxExperts used for authority positioning
   - Agency-specific tags (ATOTips, CRATips) show local knowledge

3. **Combination Strategy Works**
   - Real posts use 7-13 hashtags (not 5-9 as claimed)
   - Mix of broad expat tags + country-specific + topic-specific
   - Lifestyle tags (#ExpatLife, #LivingAbroad) included for community engagement

4. **Content-Hashtag Alignment Is Critical**
   - Hashtags match the EXACT content topic
   - No generic stuffing with #Tax or #Finance
   - Hyper-relevant tags only
