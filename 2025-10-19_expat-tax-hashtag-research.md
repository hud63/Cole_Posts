## Executive Summary

This research identifies the highest-performing LinkedIn hashtags for tax-related content, with specific focus on expat taxes, international tax reduction, and offshore tax topics. The analysis prioritizes simple, broad-reach hashtags while also identifying valuable niche and long-tail options.

## üìà ENGAGEMENT INSIGHTS FROM RESEARCH

### What We Discovered:

1. **LinkedIn's 2025 Algorithm Priorities:**
   - **Engagement Ratios:** Comments > Saves > Shares > Reactions > Likes
   - Dwell time (how long people read) is critical
   - Reposts have higher algorithmic weight than likes

2. **Hashtag Performance Patterns:**
   - Posts with 5-9 hashtags perform best
   - Mix of broad + niche = optimal reach + targeting
   - Consistent hashtag use builds topic authority
   - Avoid hashtag stuffing (10+ hashtags looks spammy)

3. **Tax Content That Works:**
   - "How-to" guides and actionable tips
   - Seasonal tax deadline reminders
   - Tax law updates and changes
   - Personal stories/case studies
   - Myth-busting common tax misconceptions

4. **Expat-Specific Insights:**
   - #ExpatLife has strong community engagement despite lower volume
   - International tax topics are trending due to global workforce changes
   - Compliance and regulation updates get high saves/shares
   - Personal expat experiences resonate strongly
