## Why Próspera

Próspera is a special economic zone on the island of Roatán, Honduras, operating under a forward-thinking governance model designed for global entrepreneurs and remote professionals. ## Application Process

1. **Apply Remotely:** Submit your application and KYC documents online.
    
2. **Obtain e-Residency:** Activate your Próspera e-Residency to access the jurisdiction's digital platform and governance system.
    
3. **Approval & Payment:** Once approved, pay the annual $5,000 USD Lump Sum Tax.
    
4. **Receive Certificate:** Obtain your official Próspera Tax residency Certificate.
    
5. **Set Up Your Company:** Within 60 days of residency approval, establish your Próspera company (can be done remotely).
    
6. **Visit Próspera:** Complete your required 7-day visit and receive a $1,000 USD tax refund.
